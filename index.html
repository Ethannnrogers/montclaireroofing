about

Family Owned Since 1981

The Carmailla Family started the business with the goal to provide roofing services that would benefit both the business and it's customers. The business started off with just 2 employees and has now grown to 35+ employees.

Alex and Jenny Carmailla have lived in Montclair, NJ all of their lives and plan to stay in the area to keep running the business. They both take a large role in the business as Alex is the CEO and Jenny is the CFO.
